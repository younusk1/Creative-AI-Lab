  Milestone gates matter more than dates:

  - v0.1 complete: You can chat with Gemma locally through Open WebUI and record the result of a prompt experiment.
  - v0.2 complete: One brief reliably produces a structured planning package and its history is saved.
  - v0.3 complete: One campaign can be traced from brief to storyboard and reusable prompts.
  - v0.4 complete: A human can approve a brief and trigger a reviewable, auditable handoff.

  A sensible weekly rhythm:

  - Day 1: choose one narrow deliverable.
  - Days 2–3: build the smallest working version.
  - Day 4: add tests, documentation, and an example.
  - Day 5: run it on a real brief; record what failed and adjust the next week.

  Avoid adding Metabase, more agents, image/video integrations, or model comparisons until the first campaign case study is complete. At this stage,
  a reliable simple workflow is much more valuable than a larger stack.
